DDJ-SR CONTROLLER PIONEER


...Portable two-channel controller


The DDJ-SR delivers all the superior performance features of the top-flight four-channel DDJ-SX2, including an inbuilt Serato sound card and pre-mapped controls for the included Serato DJ software.

Descrtiption



The DDJ-SR incorporates features from our pro-range include large, low-latency jog wheels and Slip Mode – which silently continues track playback during loops, scratches and reverses, bringing the music back in the right place when the DJ is done.


Features


  • 16 Performance Pads provide HOT CUE, ROLL, SLICER and SAMPLER functions New PAD PLUS provide HOT CUE ROLL, TRANS, COMBO FX, and SAMPLER ROLL Large Jog Wheels for precise scratching and mixing capabilities Built-in 2-Channel Mixer with Crossfader Reverse USB Power – The controller can be powered through the USB connection to a laptop. Built-in Sound Card (audio interface) – Simple connection to a laptop with a single USB cable. Adjustable Cross-fader Curve – Suits any mixing style. Channel Fader Start – Enables the use of the channel fader to control and play/cue tracks. Integrated Handles – The controller features grip handles on each side for added convenience.

Speacifications


  • Width: 553.6 mm
  • Height: 65.3 mm
  • Depth: 319.1 mm
  • Weight: 4.5 kg
  • Soundcard: 24 bit/44.1 kHz
  • Frequency Range: 20 - 20,000 Hz
  • Distortion: < 0.006 % (MASTER OUT)
  • Power Requirements: USB (through computer), rated output 5 V DC
  • Channels: 2
  • Deck Control: 2 / Dual
  • Jog Wheel: 132.5 mm durable aluminium jog wheel (center illumination)
  • Filter: High & Low Pass filter
  • Effects: FX engine Powered by iZotope: 10 effects
  • Hot Cues: 8
  • Manual Loop: Yes
  • Autoloop: Yes
  • Slip Mode: Yes
  • Talkover Function: Yes
  • Inputs: 1 AUX (RCA) | 1 MIC (1/4 inch Jack)
  • Outputs: 2 HEADPHONE MONITOR (1/4 inch Jack, MiniPin) | USB | 1 USB B port
