Ypoc Basic 250 features high quality and modern technology. The high brightness in its class is achieved by combining a 250W discharge lamp with a high quality coated optical system. Except the reduced set of features, it offers similar functions compared to the Ypoc 250. With 11 dichroic colors plus white, 9 static Gobos plus open, Shutter, Dimmer and a 4,5mW Laserdiode it offers the standard equipment for a washlight in his class.
